The Penguin, an Awesome Animal

Penguins are flightless birds. They live in the southern hemisphere and are very good swimmers. They are also very cute and walk funny.

Some penguin facts.
Penguins have an average sense of hearing for birds
Male penguins sometimes give pebbles as gifts to their partners
In emperor penguins, the males are solely responsible for incubation
The snazzy black and white "suits" penguins wear are for camouflage
